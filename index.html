Purchasing & Supplies

Kenya has experienced drastic changes in the business environment over the past decade. The competition in the market has increased with the advent of liberalization. Organizations, both in the public and private sectors have had to adjust and seek ways of becoming more efficient and more effective in the provision of goods and services. There is a need therefore to develop both the function and profession of purchasing and supply.
